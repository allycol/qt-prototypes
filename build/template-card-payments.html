Card Payments

If you're a Qantas Frequent Flyer or Qantas Business Direct member, you can save your credit or debit card details for the next time you book a flight and save time.

How to store your card

You can store up to three cards in total, one at a time, for future flight payments~

  1. Simply log in using your Frequent Flyer or Business Direct account before making your booking
  2. When paying for your flight, select 'Save card details'

How to update your stored card details

Frequent Flyer Members:

  • You can edit or delete stored cards by logging in to your Frequent Flyer profile
  • Select 'Your Profile' from the left hand navigation panel
  • Click the 'Saved Payment Details' section and edit or delete your stored card details

» Log in to Frequent Flyer

Qantas Business Direct Members:

  • Log in to your Business Direct profile
  • Select 'Update Your Profile' from the left hand navigation panel
  • Click the 'Payment Cards' tab and edit or delete your stored card details

» Log in to Business Direct

You can use your debit or prepaid card to avoid the Card Payment Fee* when paying for bookings departing within 7 days. Alternatively, you can use BPAY to avoid the Card Payment Fee when paying for bookings at least 7 days before departure. All other card payments (excluding Qantas UATP cards) will incur a Card Payment Fee (per passenger, per booking)+.

Pay for your flight using one of the following options:

Dynamic Currency Conversion

If you are making a booking departing from Australia, UK,USA, or Singapore using an eligible card you can choose to pay in the same currency as your card currency using our Dynamic Currency Converter.

Simply enter your eligible card details on the Pay page and your ticket price will be converted to the currency of your card. You can then choose to pay in that currency or the original currency quoted.

Dynamic Currency Conversion is available for MasterCard and VISA cards issued in: AU dollars, US dollars, Canadian dollars, Euros, GBP, NZ dollars, Hong Kong dollars, and Singapore dollars.

Dynamic Currency Conversion Conditions

  • Dynamic Currency Conversion is not available for fares booking using Qantas Points, 'Book Now, Pay Later' bookings, or if you add other products such as carbon offset and travel insurance to your flight booking. You can purchase these products with your flight booking using the quoted currency.
  • If you choose to pay in the quoted currency with a card issued in another currency, the exchange rate and any conversion fees will be determined by your card provider. If you choose to accept the Dynamic Currency Conversion offer, the conversion amount shown will include a Qantas administration charge (which is non-refundable).
  • Any ticket refunds on tickets purchased using Dynamic Currency Conversion will be made in the quoted currency, at the quoted amount and the exchange rate applied to the refund amount will be determined by your card provider.

UATP

When you use your UATP card to pay for your booking you can avoid the Card Payment Fee. Simply select UATP as your payment method.
